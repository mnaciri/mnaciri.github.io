Dog Intelligence

A scientific approach to the cognitive abilities of dogs

Dogs are very impressive. They have an incredible ability to percieve the world with their nose. This is how they recognize other dogs or even humans.

Sense of smell

Scientists guess that dogs have a sense of smell 10.000 to 100.000 times more developed than ours. Dogs have 50 times more scent receptors than ours.

Dogs are not equals about their ability to smell. Dogs with little nose like french bull dogs can't smell as well as german shepherds for example.

Dogs can smell human emotions. Of course, emotions have no scent but dogs can detect a different smell from our scent due to our emotions. For instance, it has been proved that human sweat due to sport is not the same sweat due to fear. They have a sense as precise as this difference.

They are very good to track objects or persons with their nose because they have the ability to separate scents and be focused to a precise one.

Vision

The vision of the dog is very different than ours. For example, they don't have the same spectrum of colors.

 The spectrum of colours that human perceives VS. The spectrum of colours that dog perceives

As you can see, humans has a tri-chromatic vision (red,green,blue). Dogs have a dichromatic vision (blue-purple,yellow). This difference is not a difficulty for dogs. Seeing-eye dogs, for instance, can't recognize green or red light. They know the position of the brighten light. So they know if they can croos the road or not.

Dogs cannot see as precise as us in a long distance. For example at 6 meters, dog has the same vision as human at 22 meters.

Dog communication

Dogs have a lot of way to communicate with each other. Sounds can also be used. They can bark to announce their position or growl to say that they don't want to be disturbed. A different growl can be translated as a call for assistance. They also howl to indicate their presence or to know if they are alone. That is a need of companionship. Dogs can also cry when they are very sad.

According to scientists, they can create a scent story with their urine during a walk. That's pretty impressive.

 Body language of an agressive dog

They also have a body language. For instance, a dog with a contracted back and ears backwards is an agressive dog ready to fight.

Dog also communicates with their tail. The alpha male is the dog which has his tail up. Conversely, a dog with a tail down (between the back legs) is the body language of a submissive dog.

Dog emotions

It has been scientifically proved that dogs can have complex emotions such as compassion. Some emotions that we think dogs can have are not perceived by them.

Dogs can be excited, happy, disgusted. They also have the ability to feel fear, anger, joy, suspicion, distress and affection or love.

Some very complex emotions can't be related with dogs; Such as shame, pride, guilt and contempt. Some people say (and a lot of fun videos on the internet) that dogs can be guilty. If the dog was wise, scientists hid the sausage to be sure the owner think that his dog ate the sausage. Human-dog communication

After several years of life with dogs, it is relatively easy to communicate with a dog. Dogs are very good to recognize vocal orders. But they are also able to recognize our emotions. When someone is sad, they know it and compative dogs can be very comforting.
